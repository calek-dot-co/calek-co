My approach to design

Process

Originally from the green and gold of Melbourne, I now call Berlin home. Studying Brand and Communication Design at university originally got me interested in the power of communication, but since then I have done most of my learning working hands-on in the fields of product, service, and experience design. I've now been thinking, making, and communicating for over 12 years.

I believe that by creating empathy with people and their problems, we can use the latest technology to create new & relevant experiences for the betterment of everyone’s lives. This is best unlocked with multidisciplinary teams collaborating towards a common goal. No problem or project is the same, so I use a set of core methods flexibly depending on the challenge.

In my spare time I can be found working hands on making lighting designs, cycling in the countryside, or exploring everything amazing that Europe has to offer.
