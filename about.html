About

Originally from the green and gold of Melbourne, Australia—I’ve now called Berlin (and Europe) home for over 6 years.

I’ve been thinking, making, and communicating for 12 years since I started my design journey studying Brand and Communication Design at university. However, I've have done most of my learning through working hands-on in the fields of product, service, and experience design.

I believe that by creating empathy with people and their problems, we can use creativity and technology to design new and relevant experiences that truely help. This is best unlocked with multidisciplinary teams collaborating towards a common goal. No problem or project is the same, so I use a set of core methods flexibly depending on the challenge.

Steering projects in the right direction is also important, because design should be a power for good in the world. We shouldn't just be trying to sell more stuff, but making life better for future generations by positively contributing to equality and the climate.
