Eucerin

Launching a user-centric digital eco-system to deliver life-changing skincare online in 50+ countries worldwide.

Creative lead
User research
Experience design
Digital design system

Across this 18 month project, I led a multidisciplinary team to uncover user needs, ideate solutions and deliver development ready features and services. The initial brief started with a new global .com template—relaunching Eucerin mobile-first for a new, younger audience—but quickly expanded to the full digital customer lifecycle including e-commerce & e-CRM. Pilots for the new eco-system launched in selected markets within 3 months of design start, continuing to expand with new features and countries on a regular cadence.

E.ON

Fast tracking digitalisation and sustainability with a omnichannel digital design system, bringing good energy to everyone.

Creative co-lead
Brand design vision
Digital design system

In collaboration with a branding creative director, I co-led a experience and visual design team to re-launch E.ON’s digital design system, fit for modern digital touch-points and fully bringing to life the brand’s new strategy and mission: to connect everyone to good energy. The biggest challenge with the new E.ON design system was the difference of needs between different content types as well as avoiding the pitfalls of the previous design style which required not over-indexing on E.ON Red, while still finding a range of communication styles that felt uniquely E.ON.

Brightfield

Illuminating the B2B electrical grid with a launch website for a new type of data acquisition system for the energy grid.

Creative lead
User research
Journey mapping
Responsive website design

Across the 2 months of project sprints, I led the experience design team, facilitating brand and UI collaboration to define a new digital brand. Building off each others’ success the Brightfield style was co-created and the project quickly went from interview insights to a developed website. Leveraging linear storytelling, the nuance and complexity of a new type of grid hardware project was brought to life – illuminating the once dark energy grid.
